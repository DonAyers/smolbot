# Fez - Building Design Inspiration

## Key Features:
- Distinct pixel art style with 16-bit era graphics
- Cube-shaped buildings and floating platforms
- Tall, layered towers and suspended walkways
- Vibrant, minimalistic pixel art
- Intricately designed structures

## Buildings Characteristics:
- Geometric, blocky architecture
- Floating buildings and platforms
- Ancient ruins aesthetic mixed with whimsical design
- Bridges connecting platforms
- Colorful, atmospheric lighting
- Variety in building heights and compositions

## Color Palette:
- Vibrant colors throughout
- Warm lighting for inviting atmospheres
- Cool blues and purples for mysterious areas
- Lush greens for forest/natural areas

## Perspective Mechanic:
- 3D buildings rendered in 2D
- Rotating perspective reveals different faces
- Depth created through layering and color
