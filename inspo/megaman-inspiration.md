# Megaman X - Building Design Inspiration

## Key Features:
- Futuristic cityscapes with industrial zones
- Collapsed highways and large buildings in backgrounds
- Cyberpunk urban atmosphere
- City skylines with towering skyscrapers
- Layered backgrounds with parallax scrolling
- Dynamic level backgrounds with mechanized structures

## Buildings Characteristics:
- Industrial factories and power plants
- Urban environments with depth
- Variety in building heights and shapes
- Strong vertical elements (towers, skyscrapers)
- Detailed foreground and background layers

## Color Palette:
- Dark grays and metallics for industrial areas
- Bright accent colors for energy/tech elements
- Urban blues and purples for night scenes

## References:
- Opening "Highway" stage: bustling city with collapsed structures
- Various stage backgrounds showing urban decay and futuristic tech
- Mix of destroyed and intact buildings

Source: Mega Man X gameplay screenshots and level design analysis
